Mediterranean Lentil Soup

Preserved lemon, Aleppo pepper & a bright herby oil — adapted from Rainbow Plant Life
DeviceTM6
Prep15 min
Cook45 min~75 min
Total60 min~90 min
Servings48

Ingredients

  • Soup Base
  • 200 g onion (~1 large), quartered
  • 180 g carrots (~2 medium), in chunks
  • 22 g garlic (~5 cloves)
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 18 g preserved lemon peel, chopped by hand
  • 1 tsp Aleppo pepper (or ½ tsp red pepper flakes)
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 200 g green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 700 ml vegetable broth
  • 150 ml water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 fresh thyme sprigs
  • 12 g nutritional yeast
  • 7 g salt (adjust to taste)
  • 15 ml lemon juice
  • Herby Oil
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 16 g fresh basil, dill, and/or parsley
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 2 pinches flaky sea salt
  • Optional Yogurt Sauce
  • 120 g plain vegan yogurt
  • 10 ml lemon juice
  • 1 pinch salt & pepper

Ingredients (doubled — 2 batches)

Split soup base ingredients evenly across both batches. Herby oil and yogurt sauce are made once at double quantity.

  • Soup Base (total for both bowls)
  • 400 g onion (~2 large), quartered
  • 360 g carrots (~4 medium), in chunks
  • 44 g garlic (~10 cloves)
  • 60 ml olive oil (30 ml per batch)
  • 36 g preserved lemon peel, chopped by hand
  • 2 tsp Aleppo pepper (1 tsp per batch)
  • 4 tsp ground coriander (2 tsp per batch)
  • 400 g lentils (200 g per batch)
  • 1400 ml vegetable broth (700 ml per batch)
  • 300 ml water (150 ml per batch)
  • 4 bay leaves (2 per batch)
  • 6 thyme sprigs (3 per batch)
  • 24 g nutritional yeast (12 g per batch)
  • 14 g salt (7 g per batch)
  • 30 ml lemon juice (15 ml per batch)
  • Herby Oil (serves 8)
  • 45 ml olive oil
  • 24 g fresh herbs (basil, dill, parsley)
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 2 pinches flaky sea salt
  • Optional Yogurt Sauce (serves 8)
  • 240 g plain vegan yogurt
  • 20 ml lemon juice
  • 1 pinch salt & pepper

Preparation

  1. Place carrots (180 g) and garlic (22 g) into mixing bowl. Chop 5 sec / speed 5. Scrape down. Transfer to a small bowl.
  2. Add onion (200 g). Chop 5 sec / speed 7. Scrape down.
  3. Add 30 ml olive oil. Sauté 12 min / 120°C / speed 1. Measuring cup on lid.
  4. During sauté: chop 18 g preserved lemon peel by hand. Measure out spices.
  5. Add reserved carrots/garlic and preserved lemon. Sauté 5 min / 120°C / speed 1.
  6. Add 1 tsp Aleppo pepper and 2 tsp coriander. Cook 1 min / 120°C / speed 1.
  7. Place simmering basket with bay leaves and thyme inside bowl. Add 700 ml broth, 150 ml water, 200 g lentils, black pepper. Cook 22 min / 100°C / ↻ speed 1. Measuring cup on lid.
  8. During simmer: prepare herby oil — chop herbs, combine with lemon zest, olive oil, and flaky salt. Optional: stir yogurt sauce.
  9. Lift out simmering basket, discard herbs. Stir in 12 g nutritional yeast and 7 g salt. Cook 2 min / 100°C / ↻ speed 1. Stir in 15 ml lemon juice. Taste and adjust.
  10. Ladle into bowls. Spoon herby oil generously over each serving. Dollop yogurt sauce if using. Serve with crusty bread.

Preparation (2 Bowls)

Strategy: Batch-chop all aromatics up front. Start Bowl 1 cooking. During its 12-min onion sauté + 22-min lentil simmer, prep condiments and pre-measure Bowl 2. Swap immediately when Bowl 1 finishes simmering.

Time saved vs two separate cooks: ~20 min (single chop session, condiments done during dead time, immediate Bowl 2 start).

Tips & Tricks

  • Onion browning: TM6 caps at 120°C, so the extended 12-min sauté compensates. For deeper caramelization, do onions in a pan first, then deglaze into the bowl.
  • Preserved lemons: Chop by hand — the TM6 over-processes them into mush. Whole Foods stocks Mina brand.
  • Lentil texture: Reverse mode keeps them intact. For thicker soup, remove a third, pulse 3–4 times / speed 5, then recombine.
  • Storage: Keeps 5 days refrigerated. Store herby oil separately — basil blackens by day 2. Hold the lemon juice when freezing.
  • 2-bowl strategy: ~90 min for 8 servings. The 12-min sauté + 22-min simmer in Bowl 1 gives you ~34 min to prep all condiments and measure Bowl 2 ingredients.
