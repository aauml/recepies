Spiced Vegetable Lentil Soup

Hearty, aromatic, and finished with lemon — a Thermomix one-pot meal
DeviceTM6
Prep10 min
Cook35 min~65 min
Total45 min~75 min
Servings612

Ingredients

  • 300 g brown or green lentils, rinsed
  • 200 g onions (~1 large), quartered
  • 150 g carrots (~1 medium), in chunks
  • 60 g celery (~1 stalk), in chunks
  • 15 g garlic (~3 cloves)
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 400 g canned diced tomatoes
  • 800 g vegetable stock
  • 5 g cumin, ground
  • 3 g smoked paprika
  • 2 g dried oregano
  • 1 g turmeric
  • 1 g black pepper
  • 80 g fresh kale or spinach
  • 30 ml lemon juice (~1 lemon)
  • 7 g salt (adjust to taste)

Ingredients (doubled — 2 batches)

Prep all ingredients. Split evenly into two sets before cooking. Each bowl runs at full single-batch capacity.

  • 600 g brown or green lentils, rinsed
  • 400 g onions (~2 large), quartered
  • 300 g carrots (~2 medium), in chunks
  • 120 g celery (~2 stalks), in chunks
  • 30 g garlic (~6 cloves)
  • 80 g olive oil (40 g per batch)
  • 800 g canned diced tomatoes
  • 1600 g vegetable stock
  • 10 g cumin, ground
  • 6 g smoked paprika
  • 4 g dried oregano
  • 2 g turmeric
  • 2 g black pepper
  • 160 g fresh kale or spinach
  • 60 ml lemon juice (~2 lemons)
  • 14 g salt (7 g per batch)

Preparation

  1. Place onions, carrots, celery, and garlic into mixing bowl. Chop 5 sec / speed 5. Scrape down sides with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil. Sauté 3 min / 120°C / speed 1.
  3. Add cumin, smoked paprika, oregano, turmeric, and black pepper. Cook 1 min / 120°C / speed 1 until fragrant.
  4. Add diced tomatoes. Cook 2 min / 100°C / speed 1.
  5. Add lentils and vegetable stock. Cook 25 min / 100°C / ↻ speed 1. Measuring cup on lid.
  6. Check lentils for tenderness. If needed, cook 5 min / 100°C / ↻ speed 1 more.
  7. Add kale, salt, and lemon juice. Cook 3 min / 100°C / ↻ speed 1 until greens wilt.
  8. Optional half-blend: ladle ~400 g out. Blend remaining 10 sec / speed 6. Return reserved portion, stir 10 sec / ↻ speed 1.

Preparation (2 Bowls)

Strategy: Batch-chop all veg in one go. Cook Bowl 1 fully. During its 25-min simmer, pre-measure all Bowl 2 ingredients so it starts without delay.

Time saved vs two separate cooks: ~15 min (one chop session, zero downtime between batches).

Bowl 2 · ~36 min
  1. Bowl 1 Place all onions (400 g), carrots (300 g), celery (120 g), and garlic (30 g) into mixing bowl. Chop 5 sec / speed 5. Scrape down. Transfer half to a container labelled “B2 veggies.”
  2. Bowl 1 Add 40 g olive oil. Sauté 3 min / 120°C / speed 1.
  3. Bowl 1 Add 5 g cumin, 3 g paprika, 2 g oregano, 1 g turmeric, 1 g pepper. Cook 1 min / 120°C / speed 1.
  4. Bowl 1 Add 400 g diced tomatoes. Cook 2 min / 100°C / speed 1.
  5. Bowl 1 Add 300 g lentils and 800 g stock. Cook 25 min / 100°C / ↻ speed 1. Measuring cup on lid.
  6. ⏲ During B1 simmer Pre-measure Bowl 2: 40 g oil, all spices (same amounts), 400 g tomatoes, 300 g lentils, 800 g stock, 80 g kale, 7 g salt, 30 ml lemon. Place B2 veggies into Bowl 2 (off base).
  7. Bowl 1 When done, add 80 g kale, 7 g salt, 30 ml lemon juice. Cook 3 min / 100°C / ↻ speed 1.
  8. Bowl 1 Optional half-blend: ladle 400 g out, blend 10 sec / speed 6, return, stir 10 sec / ↻ speed 1. Pour into serving pot. Remove Bowl 1.
  9. ⏲ Swap Place Bowl 2 (with veggies) onto the base. Everything is pre-measured — zero delay.
  10. Bowl 2 Add 40 g olive oil. Sauté 3 min / 120°C / speed 1.
  11. Bowl 2 Add spices. Cook 1 min / 120°C / speed 1.
  12. Bowl 2 Add 400 g tomatoes. Cook 2 min / 100°C / speed 1.
  13. Bowl 2 Add 300 g lentils and 800 g stock. Cook 25 min / 100°C / ↻ speed 1. Measuring cup on lid.
  14. Bowl 2 Add 80 g kale, 7 g salt, 30 ml lemon juice. Cook 3 min / 100°C / ↻ speed 1.
  15. Bowl 2 Optional half-blend same as step 8. Combine with first batch.

Tips & Tricks

  • Toasting spices in hot oil before adding liquid unlocks their full aroma.
  • Reverse mode (↻) is critical during simmering — it stirs the lentils without breaking them apart.
  • The half-blend creates a creamy body while keeping chunks. Skip for rustic, go full blend for smooth.
  • This soup improves overnight. Fridge 4 days, freeze 3 months.
  • 2-bowl strategy: ~75 min total for 12 servings. Use Bowl 1's 25-min simmer to pre-measure everything for Bowl 2.
